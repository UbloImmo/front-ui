## Usage

### Colors

Like the `Badge` component, Chip takes **raw** colors _(e.g. `primary`)_ and **not** shaded colors _(e.g. `primary-light`)_ as its `color` property.
The resulting Chip's background, text and icon colors are computed based on this property.

<Canvas of={ChipStories.Colors} />

### Label and Icon

`Chip` needs a **label** to render properly. You can provide an optional **icon** with a valid `IconName`.

> Please refer to the [Icon Repository](?path=/docs/components-iconography-icon-repository--docs) for a complete list.

### Icon placement

By default, the icon in a `Chip` is positioned on the left of its label. You can easily switch icon's position to the right by setting the property `iconPlacement` to **right**.

<Canvas of={ChipStories.Placement} />

### Deleting a chip

To use the interactivity of the `Chip`, you can provide your desired callback function in the optional `onDelete` property. This will be triggered when the button on the right of the `Chip` is clicked.
This allows you to implement your trigger action to remove the selected `Chip`.

> Give it a try and delete those chips below !

<Canvas of={ChipStories.WithDelete} />

#### Make a chip undeletable

Sometimes chip should not be deletable.
You can disable the interaction and hide the delete button by setting the `disabled` property to `true`.

<Canvas of={ChipStories.Disabled} />
