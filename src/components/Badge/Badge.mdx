## Usage

### Colors

Badge takes **raw** colors _(e.g. `primary`)_ and **not** shaded colors _(e.g. `primary-light`)_ as its `color` property.

The resulting Badge's background, text and icon colors are computed based on this property.

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Colors} />

### Shades

Badge adapt their container's background color, text color and icon color based on their `shade` property.

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Shades} />

### Icons

Through its `icon` property, Badge accepts any valid Icon name, and passes it directly to the underlying Icon component.
Rendering `icon` property in the Badge is optionnal
